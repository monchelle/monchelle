# B2B Happy Customer Score (HCS) Dashboard

This project summarizes weekly customer satisfaction performance for IKEA for Business B2B queues (chat, voice, resolutions, and sales). I designed and led this initiative to align four team leaders around a shared view of customer experience and coaching priorities.

## Objectives
- Track Happy Customer Score (HCS) by channel and coworker
- Identify trends in knowledge, manners, and trust scores
- Surface top feedback themes and coaching actions
- Support weekly leadership discussions and targeted coaching

## Key Features
- Weekly dashboards summarizing:
  - Overall HCS by channel (chat vs. voice)
  - Survey participation rates
  - Coworker-level HCS performance
  - Knowledge, Manners, Trust (KMT) scores
  - Top feedback themes and coaching actions
- Correlation analysis between KMT and HCS to prioritize coaching focus
- Actionable coaching notes for leaders to improve call quality and customer outcomes

## My Role
- Designed the dashboard structure and metrics
- Analyzed survey and performance data weekly
- Identified trends and outliers for coaching
- Facilitated alignment across four B2B leaders on priorities and actions
